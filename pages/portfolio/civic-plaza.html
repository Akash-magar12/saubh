Civic Plaza

Case study

This project explores a careful balance between structure, light and material. Our team developed a concept rooted in context and climate, then advanced it through rigorous drawings and details. The plan prioritizes simple circulation and comfortable proportions while the envelope controls glare and heat. Natural textures and a restrained palette deliver calm, while custom joinery brings a crafted precision. Performance targets informed each decision, from shading to ventilation. The result is a space that feels generous and usable every day, with flexibility for future change.
